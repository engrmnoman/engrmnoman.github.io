About Me

Hello! I'm Noman

I'm a Post-MS Researcher at DGIST's Information and Communication Engineering Research Center, specializing in GPU-accelerated algorithms for real-time medical imaging systems.

My research focuses on developing high-performance computing solutions for ultrasound and optical coherent imaging using CUDA, C++, and OpenGL. I'm passionate about leveraging parallel computing to push the boundaries of real-time medical diagnostics.

With a background in software engineering and extensive experience in machine learning, I bridge the gap between theoretical algorithms and practical, deployable medical imaging systems.
